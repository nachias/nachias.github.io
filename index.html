Nachias Fashion Pvt Ltd

Nachias Fashion Pvt Ltd are manufactures of Men’s clothing under the brand names of “Casino” and “Colour Tree”. The company has made a place in the fiercely competitive formal clothing space by strategically providing customer-centric service and quality at affordable prices. Since inception the company have grown steadily to a 300-strong workforce integrated ably with modern infrastructure. An experienced team of employees, motivated by a closely involved, family-run management has allowed the company to become a leading brand in South India.
